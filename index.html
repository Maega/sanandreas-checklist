Exploits

In the Windows Store version of the game, this mission can be exploited to skip all the way to End of the Line (the final mission). A detailed explanation of this exploit can be seen in this YouTube video.

Bugs

In the Definitive Edition, it's possible to buy an expensive haircut without being able to afford it during this mission, causing the player to go into negative balance and being unable to afford a meal at Well Stacked Pizza.

Exploits

The Spray Can you get during this mission has a much higher ammo capacity than regular pickups and be used to immediately spray all the tags in Los Santos.

Exploits

After finishing the mission, Sweet's Greenwood will remain fireproof until it despawns.

Notes

The Ballas will become hostile to CJ after this mission.

Exploits

Before getting in the car at the beginning of the mission, you are immune to getting a wanted level and can freeroam the entire map. This means you can venture to locked parts of the San Andreas and pickup some of the more powerful weapons in the game early. You cannot start any missions or do anything that can't normally be done while on a mission, but weapon spawns and collectibles are freely available.

Bugs

In the PC version of the game, a long system uptime can cause the lowrider mini-game to lag or work improperly. Rebooting your computer fixes this issue which is caused by the game tracking the time between each arrow using system uptime in seconds. This variable can overflow, resulting in the bug.

Exploits

You can skip the dance battle portion of this mission by recruiting a gang member to enter the van on your behalf then shooting them which will cause the them to fall out of the van, leaving the door open for you to enter. A more detailed guide on how to pull off this exploit can be seen in this YouTube video.

Missable Trophy

The trophy/achievement Assassin can be missed during this mission. To get it, you'll need to stealth kill all eight guards in Madd Dogg's house without being detected.

Exploits

Damage-Proof PCJ-600
During the second part of the mission, you can acquire Freddy's PCJ-600 which is immune to bullets, fire, explosions, melee attacks and collisions. To do this, ring Freddy's doorbell to trigger the chase sequence. Then, walk on-foot to where Freddy is waiting for you until he starts firing. Keep Freddy in your line of sight and kill OG Loc to fail the mission. After failing the mission, you'll be able to knock Freddy off his bike and claim it as your own.

Exploits

Damage-Proof Sanchez
If you submerge the Sanchez given to you and then fail the mission, you can use a sawed-off shotgun or other high impact weapon to push the bike out of the water and claim it as your own. The bike cannot be damaged by bullets, fire, explosions, melee attacks or collisions and will remain yours as long as you save it in a garage.

Exploits

Fire/Explosion Proof Vehicles
This mission can be exploited to add the FP & EP properties to any vehicle.
